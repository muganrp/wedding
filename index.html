About Us

Katryna & Ryan's journey together began in Eau Claire, WI brought together by distance running. Moving west after graduation, their relationship continues to grow in new and exciting ways. While work keeps them tied to the city, they spend every chance they can exploring what the Northwest has to offer. Backpacking to untouched alpine lakes, backcountry skiing, and sleeping in the back of their subaru before pre-dawn trail runs are all common themes in their weekend agenda.

Wedding Details

We are excited to share the beautiful PNW with you. Located on the flanks of Mt Hood, Timberline is a historic lodge surrounded by recreational opportunities.

For lodging, we have a block of rooms at Timberline itself on hold until June 10, 2019. To inquire, please call the Reservations Department (Williams & Mugan Wedding Block) at 503-272-3410. The lodge is an ideal location giving you direct access to the mountain and surrounding trails without having to get in your car. That said, this isn't the Four Seasons at Vail. The lodge does not have AC and while the average high for August is 70Â°, a hot weekend is possible.

Numerous other lodging opportunities are available in the town of Government Camp, seven miles down the road. For a traditional hotel option, check out the Best Western Mt. Hood Inn. Ask for the discounted wedding rate. Also in town, Timberline also has condos available. Other lodging opportunities like Airbnb, VRBO, ect are available here as well.

Regardless of where you decide, it is critical you act early as summmer is a busy time in the area!

Things To Do

Hiking

The most accessible activity is hiking! Late summer is the perfect time to enjoy the high alpine on foot as the trails will be snow free and flowers will be blooming. There are no shortage of trails leaving directly from Timberline Lodge and the surrounding area.

Skiing

Yes, there is skiing opportunity on the lift serviced Plamer snowfield all summer long. Of course this depends on a strong winter, but even an average winter will provide enough snow for some summer turns! #PrayForSnow

Wineries & Breweries

Struggling up a mountain not your thing? Enjoy the area at a more leisurely pace and enjoy some local brew or tour one of the many wineries OR has to offer. Checkout Mt Hood Brewing right in town!

And Much More!
